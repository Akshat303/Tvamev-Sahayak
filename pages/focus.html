What we do

त्वमेव सहायक

Advocate Mental Health

We aim to destigmatize mental health by raising awareness about it.

Reduce Illiteracy

We aim to reduce illiteracy by educating about crucial social issues.

Educate People

We aim to educate people about prevalent issues like dowry, rape and abuse.

Advertise Sustainable Lifestyle

We aim to promote healthy lifestyle choices amongst the general public.

Support Women's Rights

We aim to support and raise awareness about women's rights and rights of people with disabilities.

Support LGBTQ+ community

We aim to support and be an ally of the LGBTQ+ community by raising awareness about them.
